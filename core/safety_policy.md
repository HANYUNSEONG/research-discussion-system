# Safety and Data Policy

RDS may be used with local research materials, but each AI surface has its own data handling policy. The project owner is responsible for choosing appropriate tools.

## Do Not Use Without Institutional Approval

- patient-identifiable data
- regulated clinical data
- protected health information
- confidential collaborator data covered by contract
- unpublished sensitive data that should not leave approved systems

## Default Practice

- de-identify sensitive data before discussion
- record when data is anonymized or synthetic
- keep raw regulated data outside agent-accessible folders unless explicitly allowed
- mark AI-generated hypotheses as hypotheses
- require human validation before treating AI interpretation as evidence
