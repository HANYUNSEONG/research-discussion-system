## Discussion Cues

These are not setup questions. Use them only when relevant during a discussion.

- What research question and population are central?
- What study design is used?
- What data collection method is used?
- What coding scheme, survey instrument, or model matters?
- What ethical or consent constraints exist?

## Discussion Emphasis

- construct validity
- sampling
- coding decisions
- alternative explanations
- limitations
